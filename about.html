Who we are

A small studio
with cinematic obsession.

CINEXD was founded in Jammu, J&K by a two-person team that believes every business deserves to look like it belongs on a billboard, a streamer, or the front of a feed.

We specialise in commercial and promotional video — from car showrooms and restaurants to hotels, brand commercials and social-first content. We obsess over the small things: the timing of a cut, the warmth of a grade, the silence before a beat drops.

If you're a regional business with a story worth telling — we'd love to be the ones to tell it.

Our Story

It started with a single reel for a friend's restaurant in Old Jammu — three takes, one mounted phone, and a song we still love.

That reel went semi-viral the same week. People started asking how we did it, what kit, how soon we could come over. We answered with more films.

Today, CINEXD makes commercials for car showrooms, brand films for hotels, and reels that get small businesses noticed. The kit's better. The standards are higher. The mission stays the same: make the work feel like cinema.
